TrashTab ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App handles and how.

## Summary

- The App does **not collect**, **store**, or **share** any personally identifiable information.
- All your debt records are stored **locally on your device** only.
- No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.
- The App does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All bill records, including debtor names, amounts, and dates, are stored exclusively on your device
- Data is stored using the device's local storage system (UserDefaults/File System)
- Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it

### Export Functionality
- The Export feature creates JSON files containing your debt records
- These files are saved to your device's local storage or shared through iOS share sheet
- You have full control over where and how you share these exported files

### Import Functionality
- The Import feature reads JSON files from your device's storage
- Only files you explicitly select are processed
- No automatic scanning or reading of your files occurs

## Permissions

### No Special Permissions Required
- The App does not require access to Photos, Camera, Contacts, or Location
- The only file access occurs when you explicitly use Import/Export features

## Data Security

### Device Security
- Your data is protected by your device's built-in security measures
- We recommend using device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID for additional protection

### No Server Storage
- Since no data is transmitted to servers, there are no server-side security risks
- Your information remains within the security boundary of your iOS device

## Third-Party Services

### No Third-Party Integration
- The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or cloud platforms
- No data is shared with advertising networks, analytics services, or social media platforms

## Children's Privacy

- The App is not directed to children under 13
- The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page. Significant changes will be noted with updated "Last updated" dates.
